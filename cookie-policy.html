Cookie Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use strictly necessary cookies to ensure the proper functionality of the NexusSend developer dashboard and API authentication gateways.

2. How We Use Them

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics pixels. The only cookies set by NexusSend are `session_id` and `oauth_state` which are required to prevent CSRF attacks during the Google OAuth login flow.

3. Managing Cookies

Because our cookies are strictly necessary for security and API functionality, disabling them in your browser will prevent you from accessing the NexusSend dashboard.
