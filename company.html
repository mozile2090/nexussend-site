About Us

NexusSend is built by a team of systems engineers dedicated to solving infrastructure bottlenecks in high-volume programmatic communication. We specialize in API orchestration, headless browser automation, and secure OAuth credential handling.

Careers

Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

We process data entirely in memory. Payload obfuscation and routing are executed without writing plain-text content to disk, ensuring compliance with global data protection standards.

Cookie Policy

NexusSend uses strictly necessary session cookies for dashboard authentication and load-balancing our proxy networks. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies on our platform.
